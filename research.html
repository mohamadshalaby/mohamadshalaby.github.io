    Research Interests

    • Investigating the microscopic (plasma-kinetic) foundations of GeV Cosmic Ray (CR) proton transport theories and their dynamic influence on stellar and galactic evolution.
    • Leveraging advanced computational techniques, including Particle-in-Cell (PIC) and Finite-Volume solvers, to study the evolutionary intricacies of Hydrodynamic (HD) and Magnetohydrodynamic (MHD) equations.
    • Exploring electron and ion acceleration mechanisms in astrophysical shocks to unravel their underlying physics.
    • Investigating the nonlinear evolution of astrophysical plasma instabilities across different environments.
