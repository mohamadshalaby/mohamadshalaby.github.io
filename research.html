    Research Interests

    • Investigating the microscopic (plasma-kinetic) foundations of GeV Cosmic Ray (CR) proton transport theories and their dynamic influence on both stellar and galactic evolution
    • I leverage advanced computational techniques, including Particle-in-Cell (PIC) and Finite-Volume solvers, to delve into the evolutionary intricacies of Hydrodynamic (HD) and Magnetohydrodynamic (MHD) equations
    • Exploration of electron and ion acceleration mechanisms within astrophysical shocks, aiming to unravel their underlying complexities
    • Investigating the nonlinear evolution of diverse astrophysical plasma instabilities
